What We Deliver

Underground to
surface, fully covered.

Three service categories built around a working mine's full lifecycle — from ground support deep underground to the earthworks and civils on the surface above it.

01

Underground Mining Services

Ground-support engineering and the ongoing underground work that keeps a working section safe, clean and productive.

Rock Engineering Consultancy

Ground control assessments and support design for safe, stable working areas.

Primary & Secondary Support Installations

Timely, spec-accurate installation of primary and secondary ground support.

Sweepings & Vampings

Face and gully clean-up that keeps working areas productive and safe.

Haulage Maintenance

Keeping ore and waste haulage routes running reliably, shift after shift.

Reclamation

Recovering value from remnant and previously worked-out areas.

Equipping

Fitting and commissioning the underground infrastructure a section needs to run.

02

Contract Mining Services

A full mining contract, run end-to-end, matched to your orebody and your method of choice.

Conventional Mining

Drill-and-blast production mining run by an experienced, engineer-led crew.

Mechanised Mining

Mechanised production methods scaled to your section's layout and targets.

03

Civil Engineering Services

The surface and structural works that support a mine — built to the same standard as the work underground.

Underground Construction

Shaft, tunnel and chamber construction executed to engineering specification.

Earth Works

Bulk earthworks, grading and site preparation for mining and civil projects.

Rubble & Waste Removal

Efficient clearing and disposal of rubble and waste material from site.

Mining & Civil Construction

Professional construction and infrastructure services supporting mining and civil engineering projects.
