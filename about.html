Our Story

Vonisanani Mining and Civils is a 100% black owned and managed company founded by Mining Engineer Ntsako Magamana.

His experience spans the mining industry over the horizons of Rock Engineering, Contract Mining and Civil Engineering Works — giving the company a rare, first-hand understanding of a project from design through to delivery.

A passion to serve the industry's needs is what places the entity at a competitive advantage in providing dedicated expert mining solutions, with a focus primarily on a high performance culture and utmost client satisfaction.
